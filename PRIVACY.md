# Privacy policy

**JsonUI Helper** collects **no telemetry** and makes **no network requests**.

All processing happens locally inside your VSCode instance. Files that the extension reads:

- `jui.config.json`, `.jsonui-type-map.json`, `.jsonui-doc-rules.json` at workspace root.
- Files under `layouts_directory`, `spec_directory`, `styles_directory`, `images_directory` as configured by `jui.config.json`.
- The specification JSON files bundled under `vendor/` (read-only, shipped with the extension).

No data leaves your machine.
